## 3. Accepted architectural vision

The Project Owner accepts the following high-level model as the target Vision Architecture of VistaRoom AI:

1. The authoritative Strategic Tracks A–H remain the sole strategic track structure.
2. Strategic Track and Major Module are different governance concepts.
3. The current Primary Active Module remains:

   `Bounded Room Understanding / Spatial Perception`

4. The project continues under Module-Completion-First governance.
5. Future Major Modules are positioned within the authoritative Strategic Tracks rather than creating a second competing track system.
6. Cross-cutting foundations apply across relevant present and future modules.
7. Real User Data Enablement is treated as a governed gate, not as a duplicate privacy-owning product track.
8. External catalogues, commerce, marketplace, pricing, availability, delivery, and contractor capabilities remain integration-dependent.
9. Current architecture and target full-platform vision remain explicitly separated.

The following architectural forms are accepted and are not reopened by this decision:

`kitchen_living_room` is an active named Composite Space Profile composed of:

### 5.2 Bedroom specializations

The following are active specializations of the base Canonical Space Type `bedroom`:

- `primary_bedroom`;
- `guest_bedroom`;
- `children_room`.

Specialization status does not mean deferred, optional, inactive, or partially supported status.

A future separately authorized Root Impact Assessment may determine exact Layer 1 identifiers, registry placement, existing-identity reuse, detailed design rules, applicability, evaluation requirements, and downstream document impact. It may not silently reverse the Owner-confirmed composite/specialization form.

## 6. Accepted cross-cutting foundations

Revision 3 is accepted with the following Owner-confirmed cross-cutting foundations:

1. **Color & Material Integration Foundation**
2. **Diagnosability & Explainability Foundation**
3. **Security & Privacy Foundation**
4. **Bilingual Localization Foundation — EN + RU**

The bilingual foundation is governed by the following mandatory rules:

- English is the canonical internal language.
- Russian is a full derived locale.
- The platform has one global language switch.
- English fallback is mandatory when a Russian translation is unavailable.
- The entire platform is bilingual from the first day of each authorized capability implementation.

`Controlled Learning & Continuous Improvement` remains a proposed future foundation until a separate explicit Owner Decision authorizes or accepts it.

## 7. Diagnosability and Security sequencing

The Project Owner accepts the sequencing reflected in Revision 3:

1. minimal Diagnosability and Security compatibility foundations are embedded in relevant current Spatial Perception successors;
2. the current Spatial Perception architecture cycle is completed;
3. Full AI Brain Diagnosability Architecture is prepared in a separate governance cycle;
4. Full Security Architecture is prepared in a separate governance cycle;
5. a mandatory Diagnosability–Security cross-check is performed;
6. later production-facing modules proceed according to separate authorization and Module-Completion-First governance.

The full Diagnosability and Security architecture cycles must not be postponed until several unrelated future product modules have already been implemented.
